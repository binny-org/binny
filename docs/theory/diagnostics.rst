|logo| Diagnostics and validation
=================================

To compare and validate binning schemes, Binny provides several diagnostic
quantities, including:

- Bin counts
- Effective redshifts
- Overlap and similarity metrics

These diagnostics are designed to highlight differences between binning
choices that may not be apparent from bin edges alone.
