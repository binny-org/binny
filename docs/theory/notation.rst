|logo| Notation
===============

Throughout the documentation, we use the following notation:

- Redshift is denoted by :math:`z`
- The redshift distribution of a sample is :math:`n(z)`
- Bin edges are denoted :math:`z_i^{\mathrm{min}}`, :math:`z_i^{\mathrm{max}}`
- The indicator function for bin membership is :math:`W_i(z)`
